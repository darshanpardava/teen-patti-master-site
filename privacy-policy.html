Teen Patti Master Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Teen Patti Master Privacy Policy. Your privacy is of paramount importance to us, and we are committed to protecting the personal information of our players. This document outlines the types of information we collect, how it's used, and the measures we take to protect it.

  • User-Provided Information: This includes information you provide when registering for an account, such as your username, email address, and contact details.

  • Usage Data: We collect information about your interactions with our game, such as game progress, scores, and achievements.

Use of Information

The information we collect helps us to:

  • Enforce our terms and conditions and combat fraud or other illegal or infringing activities.

Sharing of Information

Teen Patti Master does not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • With consent: For example, when you agree to our sharing your information with other third parties for their own marketing purposes.
  • For external processing: We provide personal information to our affiliates or other trusted businesses or persons to process it for us, based on our instructions and in compliance with our Privacy Policy and any other appropriate confidentiality and security measures.
  • For legal reasons: We will share personal information if we believe that access, use, preservation, or disclosure of the information is reasonably necessary to meet any applicable law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request.

Data Security

We take data security seriously at Teen Patti Master. We implement a variety of security measures, including encryption and authentication tools, to help protect and maintain the security, integrity, and availability of your personal information.

Data Retention

Teen Patti Master retains personal data for as long as necessary to provide the services, support your account, resolve disputes, establish legal defenses, conduct audits, pursue legitimate business purposes, and comply with applicable laws.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, delete, or restrict the use of your personal information. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our support address provided within the game.

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time. We will post any Privacy Policy changes on this page and, if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice.
